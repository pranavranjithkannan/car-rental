Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I make a reservation?

    To make a reservation, please visit our reservation page.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time without penalty. If you cancel within 24 hours of your pickup time, you will be charged a cancellation fee.

  • What types of cars do you offer?

    We offer a variety of cars, including sedans, SUVs, and luxury vehicles. Please visit our fleet page for more information.

  • How do I pay for my rental?

    We accept all major credit cards and debit cards. You can also pay with cash or a check, but a credit card is required to hold the reservation.

  • Do you offer insurance?

    Yes, we offer a variety of insurance options to fit your needs. Please visit our insurance page for more information.
